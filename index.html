This agreement is for the use of a location for filming purposes.

LOCATION USE AGREEMENT

THIS LOCATION USE AGREEMENT (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of the ____ day of ____________, ______, by and between RED FOX FEATURES LLC ("PRODUCTION COMPANY"), and Lolita ("OWNER") regarding the property located at:
550 River Road, Chatham, NJ (the "LOCATION")
1.
Purpose
1.1
The PRODUCTION COMPANY wishes to use the LOCATION for the purpose of filming the short narrative film titled MIMIC and the OWNER agrees to grant such use under the terms outlined below.
2.
Dates & Access
2.1
The PRODUCTION COMPANY shall have access to the LOCATION from April 25, 2024 through April 27, 2024(Through the Morning of the 28th as these are overnight shoots), for the purposes of set-up, pre-lighting, filming, insert shots, and breakdown.
2.2
Access hours will remain [FLEXIBLE/SPECIFIC] during these dates, with exact timing to be coordinated with the OWNER in advance.
3.
Property Preparation and Condition
3.1
The OWNER agrees to remove personal belongings, boxes, and any clutter from all rooms to be used for filming and rooms designated for crew and talent storage prior to the PRODUCTION COMPANY's arrival on April 25, 2024.
3.2
The PRODUCTION COMPANY plans to bring in a cleaning crew to vacuum, mop, and generally clean all filming areas, which requires these areas to be clear of personal items and debris.
3.3
If the LOCATION is not sufficiently cleared of clutter as described in Section 3.1 upon the PRODUCTION COMPANY's arrival, and the PRODUCTION COMPANY must spend significant time (more than 2 hours) clearing or rearranging items, the PRODUCTION COMPANY may request a partial refund of up to $1,000 from the location fee to compensate for lost preparation time.
3.4
The PRODUCTION COMPANY agrees to conduct a walk-through of the LOCATION prior to filming to document existing conditions via photo and/or video.
3.5
The PRODUCTION COMPANY shall be responsible for any damage caused during production and will repair or reimburse for any harm beyond normal wear and tear within 7 days of completion of filming. The OWNER agrees not to hold the PRODUCTION COMPANY responsible for pre-existing conditions documented during the walk-through.
4.
Insurance & Liability
4.1
The PRODUCTION COMPANY will maintain Commercial General Liability Insurance with appropriate coverage during all production activities at the LOCATION.
4.2
A Certificate of Insurance (COI) naming the OWNER as an additional insured party will be provided prior to the first day of access.
4.3
In the event of unforeseen circumstances (e.g. natural disasters, public health emergencies), both parties agree to work together in good faith to reschedule or adjust plans accordingly.
5.
Cancellation
5.1
Due to the significant planning, hiring, and purchasing that occurs ahead of production, the parties acknowledge that this Agreement is considered firm upon signing.
5.2
In the unlikely event the OWNER needs to withdraw availability after signing, the parties agree to work together in good faith to resolve the situation. However, if withdrawal results in unrecoverable costs to the PRODUCTION COMPANY (such as lost crew, non-refundable rentals, or location replacements), the OWNER may be asked to help cover those direct losses.
5.3
The PRODUCTION COMPANY remains committed to respectful communication and flexibility wherever possible.
6.
Compensation
6.1
The PRODUCTION COMPANY agrees to pay the OWNER a flat fee of $5,000 USD for use of the LOCATION for filming the short film MIMIC.
6.2
Payment will be [PAYMENT TIMING] and made via [PAYMENT METHOD]. Cash payments may be delivered in person before or on the first day of the shoot to [RECIPIENT NAME/TITLE].
6.3
Given the nature of the production schedule and commitments, this payment is considered non-refundable except in the rare case of a force majeure event (see Section 4.3) or a material breach by the PRODUCTION COMPANY.
7.
Owner's Presence & Credit
7.1
The OWNER or OWNER'S representative has the right to be present during filming, provided they do not interfere with production activities and coordinate their presence with the PRODUCTION COMPANY in advance.
7.2
The PRODUCTION COMPANY may, at its discretion, provide a "Special Thanks" credit to the LOCATION or OWNER in the end credits of the Film.
8.
Miscellaneous
8.1
This Agreement is binding upon signing by both parties.
8.2
Any changes to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
8.3
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York State.
